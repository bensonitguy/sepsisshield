# Sepsis-3 Definition and Diagnostic Criteria

## Current Definition (2016)

**Sepsis** is defined as a **life-threatening organ dysfunction caused by a dysregulated host response to infection**.

This replaces the older SIRS-based criteria. The key shift is from inflammation-based criteria to organ dysfunction.

## SOFA Score (Sequential Organ Failure Assessment)

Organ dysfunction is identified as an **acute change in total SOFA score ≥ 2 points** from baseline.

| System | Score 0 | Score 1 | Score 2 | Score 3 | Score 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Respiration** (PaO2/FiO2 mmHg) | ≥400 | 300–399 | 200–299 | 100–199 | <100 |
| **Coagulation** (Platelets ×10³/μL) | ≥150 | 100–149 | 50–99 | 20–49 | <20 |
| **Liver** (Bilirubin mg/dL) | <1.2 | 1.2–1.9 | 2.0–5.9 | 6.0–11.9 | >12 |
| **Cardiovascular** (MAP or vasopressors) | MAP ≥70 | MAP <70 | Dopamine <5 or dobutamine | Dopamine 5–15 or norepi ≤0.1 | Dopamine >15 or norepi >0.1 |
| **CNS** (GCS) | 15 | 13–14 | 10–12 | 6–9 | <6 |
| **Renal** (Creatinine mg/dL) | <1.2 | 1.2–1.9 | 2.0–3.4 | 3.5–4.9 | >5 |

**A SOFA score ≥ 2 associated with infection indicates sepsis.**

## Quick SOFA (qSOFA) — Bedside Screening Tool

qSOFA does NOT require lab values. It is used for **rapid screening outside the ICU**.

Criteria (1 point each):
- **Altered mentation** — Glasgow Coma Score <15
- **Respiratory rate** ≥ 22 breaths/min
- **Systolic blood pressure** ≤ 100 mmHg

**qSOFA score ≥ 2 = high risk; escalate to full SOFA assessment and senior physician review immediately.**

## Septic Shock Criteria

Septic shock = Sepsis PLUS:
- **Vasopressor requirement** to maintain MAP ≥65 mmHg, AND
- **Serum lactate >2 mmol/L** in the absence of hypovolemia

Hospital mortality for septic shock exceeds 40%.

## Vital Sign Thresholds for Clinical Alert

| Vital Sign | Normal | Concern | Sepsis Risk |
|---|---|---|---|
| Heart Rate (bpm) | 60–100 | >100 | >110 |
| Systolic BP (mmHg) | 90–140 | <100 | <90 |
| Respiratory Rate (/min) | 12–20 | >20 | >22 |
| SpO2 (%) | ≥96 | 92–95 | <92 |
| Temperature (°C) | 36.5–37.5 | >38 or <36 | >38.3 or <36 |
| Lactate (mmol/L) | <2.0 | 2–4 | >4 |
| WBC (K/μL) | 4.5–11 | 11–15 or <4 | >12 or <4 |
| Consciousness | Alert | Voice | Pain/Unresponsive |
